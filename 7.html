Kids Club

The Little Mates Kids Club encourages children ages 12 and under to explore maritime history in fun and exciting ways. Through hands on activities, stories and educational programs, Little Mates are able to learn first hand what it’s like to be a part of Niagara’s Crew and all about Erie’s rich maritime history.

* Free admission to the museum (with an adult member or paying adult)
* Birthday card signed by the Captain and Crew
* Official Little Mates Kids Club t-shirt

Kids Club members will also be invited to family-friendly events like:

* An exciting Lunch with the Captain of the U.S. Brig Niagara
* Discovery Day, Storytime at the Maritime and Christmas Tree Ship
* And much more!!
