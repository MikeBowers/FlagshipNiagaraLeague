Wintertime at the Maritime

The 4th Annual Wintertime at the Maritime.
Friday, February 17, 2017
6-10 pm
Erie Maritime Museum Outdoor Plaza

Cabin fever got you down? This unique outdoor event will include live music, heated tents, hors d’oeuvres, warm bar on Niagara and vodka ice bar, the Funky Winter hat Contest and much more!

Be sure to bundle up and get ready to sip warm beverages by the cozy fire pits or stay warm in our heated tents. It’s sure to be an experience you’ll never forget!

Event Tickets in (in advance):

Couples Rate: $100 ($24 tax-deductible) per couple. Includes admission for two (2) people and three (3) drink vouchers per person.



Individual: $60 ($22 tax-deductible) per person. Includes three (3) drink vouchers.
