Storytime at the Maritime

The last Monday of every month @ 10:00 am

Once a month, a special guest reader will present a maritime themed book geared toward children ages 3-7 years old. Storytime at the Maritime is a fun, educational, hands-on activity that engages children in pre-k learning objectives like math, science, art and social studies.

Each story is read by a guest speaker from the local community: “Lance the Lighthouse Keeper” or “Oliver Hazard Perry”.

The event is free and open to the public.

For more information contact Cristy at 814.452.2744 ext 226 or ccook@flagshipniagara.org.
