Become a Member

A membership to the Flagship Niagara League helps to ensure that one of America’s great maritime treasures is preserved for years to come. While we provide several benefits to our members including discounted rates and free museum admission, the single greatest benefit to membership is the satisfaction of knowing the doors to the Erie Maritime Museum remain open and the U.S. Brig Niagara continues to sail because of people like you. DON’T GIVE UP THE SHIP!

A membership to the Flagship Niagara League helps to ensure that one of America’s great maritime treasures is preserved for years to come. While we provide several benefits to our members including discounted rates and free museum admission, the single greatest benefit to membership is the satisfaction of knowing the doors to the Erie Maritime Museum remain open and the U.S. Brig Niagara continues to sail because of people like you. DON’T GIVE UP THE SHIP!

Basic Membership Benefits:

  • FREE One-Year admission to the Erie Maritime Museum and the U.S. Brig Niagara when in port
  • Discounted early-bird tickets to our special events
  • FREE subscription to Niagara News and e-newsletter
  • 15% off in our Gift Shop and online store
  • Invitation to our community enrichment events
  • Member discount on Niagara Day Sails

 

Membership Levels:

Senior Individual – $35.00

Basic Benefits for those age 62 and over.

 

Individual – $40.00

Basic Benefits

 

Senior Couple – $45.00

Basic Benefits plus unlimited admission for (2) named senior adults and their children/ family under the age of 18 living at home, PLUS grandchildren 12 and under receive a free membership to the Little Mates Kids Club.

 

Family – $50.00

Basic Benefits plus unlimited admission for (2) named adults and their children/ family under the age of 18 living at home, PLUS children 12 and under receive a free membership to the Little Mates Kids Club.

 

Captain – $100.00

Basic Benefits plus unlimited admission for two (2) named adults and their children or grandchildren under the age of 18, two (2) guests per visit to the Erie Maritime Museum, membership to the Council of American Maritime Museums (CAMM)- This entitles you to free admission to 80 maritime museums across North America and beyond, recognition in the FNL Annual Report, PLUS children 12 and under receive a free membership to the Little Mates Kids Club. ($80*)

 

Commodore – $250.00

Benefits of Captain plus three (3) guests per visit to the Erie Maritime Museum, Special Gift, PLUS children 12 and under receive a free membership to the Little Mates Kids Club. ($205*)

 

Admiral – $500.00

Benefits of Commodore plus four (4) guests per visit to the Erie Maritime Museum, PLUS children 12 and under receive a free membership to the Little Mates Kids Club. ($430*)

For more information please call Member and Donor Services at 814.452.2744 ext 222 or email.

(*Indicates tax-deductible amount)
