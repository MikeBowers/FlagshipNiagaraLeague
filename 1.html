Museum Hours and Directions

The Erie Maritime Museum/ U.S. Brig Niagara

Book A Day SailDonate

Info.

Administrative office hours are Monday - Friday, 9:00 a.m to 5:00 p.m. Please call 814.452.2744 ext 208 if you have questions.

Pricing.

Adult(12 to 65) - $10

Reduced(66+) - $8

Youth(3 to 11) - $5

Children(1 and 2) - FREE

FNL Member - FREE

* Times, prices, and availabilities are all subject to change.
* All admissions include a guided tour of the U.S. Brig Niagara when in port.
* Please call 814.452.2744 ext 208 when traveling here to see Niagara as schedule may change.
* Admission to our museum is free with a Flagship Niagara League Membership.
* School and group tours are available Year round, Monday through Saturday, only by reservation. Please visit the Group Tours page for more information.

Museum Hours of Operation

April through September:
Monday through Saturday | 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
Sunday | Noon to 5:00 p.m.

October 1 - October 31:
Monday through Saturday | 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
Sunday | Closed

November through March:
Sunday through Wednesday | Closed
Thursday through Saturday | 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

Museum Open:
* Memorial Day
* Independence Day
* Labor Day
* Christmas Eve: Museum Open (9 a.m. - 1 p.m.)
* New Year's Eve: Museum Open (9 a.m. - 1 p.m.)
** The Museum is CLOSED all other major holidays (*including the Friday after Thanksgiving and January 2nd).

Our Museum Gift Shop is open during all museum posted hours. You can reach the Gift Shop at 814.452.2744 ext 208.
